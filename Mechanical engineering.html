4.Generator Manufacturing

Basically, the generator provides a definite and alternate source of electricity at the time of power cut. 
An electrical generator is a device that generates electricity from mechanical energy, usually via electromagnetic induction.
 The diesel generators market is expected to grow from an estimated USD 13.06 Billion in 2015 to USD 16.96 Billion by 2020, 
at a CAGR of 5.4% from 2015 to 2020. Growing need for continuous power supply and increasing power outages are driving the diesel generators market worldwide.

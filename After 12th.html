Career after 12th Science

1] Medical Courses

So you are a student of science and you have done your 10+2 with PCB (Biology). Now you have the opportunity to choose the right career. Here we have some study option after 12th.

Have you ever aspire to be a part of the healthcare industry?

If, yes.

The you can opt for medical side which is more reliable and will suits your subjects. You can choose the medical field as your area of interest. 
The medical field is one of the best and prominent fields for the students having science group in 10+2 standard.

After 12th science entrance exam?

You need to go for an entrance examination to get the admission in the medical degree course.

Admission in most of the professional courses in medical fields is based on either merit or through the entrance test.
 There are different entrance exams for different courses of medical. NEET, AIIMS, JIPMER are the popular entrance exam for admission to MBBS/BDS course.

1] IIT Bombay:


IIT (Indian Institute of Technology) Bombay is the 2nd among the IITs, which was established in the year 1958 by the Government of India in collaboration with UNESCO and the Soviet Union.
 The institute has always been one of the top 10 choices for technical training among the students from engineering field.

IIT (Indian Institute of Technology) Bombay has now grown into a well recognized management Institute acquiring a unique 'institutional equity' with all its incumbent attributes such as excellent
 infrastructure, holistic curriculum and state of the art pedagogy. Being one of the top and largest technical institutes in India, it offers several Under Graduate and Post Graduate courses in the 
field of engineering. It offers 4 years full time program of Bachelor of Technology with specializations in Aerospace Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and 
Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Engineering Physics, Mechanical Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering and Materials Science.Apart from these courses, the institute also endows 
Master level program in technology i.e. M.Tech.

Admissions:

The Admission form of the College can be obtained through the Website of the College and the selection is been done through the Personal Interviews and Group Discussions.

Facilities:

IIT (Indian Institute of Technology) is located in sub-urban area of Mumbai i.e. Powai. It has a huge campus that includes 14 departments, 10 multi-disciplinary centres, and 3 schools of excellence. 
Due to its closeness to the Sanjay Gandhi National Park, the campus is extremely clean, green and mostly untouched by the pollution of the rest of the city. It has a Students' Activity Center (SAC) 
for various cultural and other extracurricular activities. The institute is well equipped with all the modern facilities. It has well maintained book library, computer lab with high speed internet connectivity. 
All the science labs are highly maintained and have been equipped with modern machineries and equipments. The institute also offers separate hostel facility for boys and girls.
